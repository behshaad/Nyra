# Soft-gated A1 assessments

Nyra's Unit Checkpoints and Final A1 Test will use soft gates: learners can continue exploring after a low score, but the relevant Unit or Level remains marked as needing review. Unit Checkpoints pass at 70%, and the Final A1 Test passes at 75%, making assessment meaningful without trapping learners in the path.

Level progression remains sequential: a later learner-available Level unlocks when the preceding Level is completed. Completing an assessment advances progression even when its score is below the passing threshold; the failed assessment contributes a needs-review signal instead of becoming a hard gate.
