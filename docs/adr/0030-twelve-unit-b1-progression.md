# Twelve-unit B1 progression

Nyra's complete B1 Level will use twelve Units, each with four regular communicative Skills and one Unit Checkpoint, followed by one Final B1 Test after Unit 12. This keeps B1 aligned with the A1 and A2 learner rhythm and progress model, while `Netzwerk-Neu-B1-Kursbuch-1.pdf` informs broad coverage, sequencing, and difficulty without copying coursebook lesson names, exercises, or wording as Nyra content.
