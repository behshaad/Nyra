# B1 Unit 1 learning guide

Nyra's first published B1 Unit will include one Persian-first Learning Guide that connects the Unit's grammar, vocabulary support, travel strategy, and comprehension strategy in a single Resource. The guide should cover `zu` infinitive, `weil` and `da`, `obwohl`, past narration, preference language, and announcement-reading or listening strategies without splitting the first rollout into multiple separate grammar, vocabulary, and strategy Resources.
