# Admin-reviewed AI scope

Nyra will not include learner-facing AI in the first MVP. AI may be introduced later for admin drafting of vocabulary, grammar notes, examples, and questions, but AI-generated content must remain draft material until reviewed and published by a human.

## Consequences

The learner runtime should use deterministic authored content, feedback, progress rules, and review behavior. AI must not grade learner answers, unlock progression, or publish educational content without human review.
