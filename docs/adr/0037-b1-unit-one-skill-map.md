# B1 Unit 1 skill map

Nyra's first published B1 Unit will organize its four regular Skills around original communicative outcomes: plan a trip and explain preferences; understand and discuss travel options; tell a holiday story in the past; and understand announcements and travel updates. This keeps Unit 1 aligned with the travel-focused opening of the primary B1 Curriculum Reference while giving Nyra learner-owned outcomes that can support B1 grammar, narration, and listening-style comprehension.
