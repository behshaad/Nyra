# B1 Unit 1 quality blueprint

Nyra's first B1 rollout will make Unit 1 fully playable with four regular communicative Skills, eight Questions per regular Skill, one Unit Checkpoint with twelve Questions, a Unit Flashcard Deck, and at least one Persian-first Learning Guide. Units 2-12 can remain Draft or In Review scaffolding until authored and reviewed, but the first published B1 Unit should meet the same learner-facing depth and quality bar established for A2.
