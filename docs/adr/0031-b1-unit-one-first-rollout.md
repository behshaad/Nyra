# B1 Unit 1 first rollout

Nyra's first B1 implementation will create the complete twelve-Unit B1 Learning Path shape, but only Unit 1 will be learner-visible as Published content at first. Later B1 Units can exist as Draft or In Review scaffolding until their Skills, Questions, Resources, and flashcards are authored and reviewed, so the product can show the B1 roadmap without presenting unfinished content as a complete Level.
