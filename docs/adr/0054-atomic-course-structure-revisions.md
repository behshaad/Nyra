# Atomic Course Structure Revisions

Hierarchy membership and ordering will be managed as a Course Structure Revision and published atomically for one Course. This keeps Course, Level, and Unit as structural containers without individual publication states while preventing direct Level, Unit, or Skill moves from partially reshaping the learner path; Skill and Question content remains owned by Skill Revisions, and map coordinates, artwork, and other visual configuration remain owned by Journey Presentation.

A structural publication must pass review and validate unique contiguous ordering, complete parent membership, referential integrity, and a coherent learner path. Learner availability is derived from the current Published Course Structure and Published Skill Revisions, while administrators can prepare and preview a Draft Course Structure without exposing it to learners.
